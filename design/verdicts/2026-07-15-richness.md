# ui-craft verdict — frontend richness pass (`feat/richness`, ADR-062)

**Date:** 2026-07-15 · **Judge:** design-cop · **Verdict: PASS ("Ship it")**
**Shots:** `design/screens/impl/richness-{match2,lineups,terminal,board}.xl.png` (HEAD)
**References:** `design/screens/terminal.png`, `design/screens/home.png`
**Scope:** baked TheSportsDB stillness imagery — national crests replace flag discs on the primary + board surfaces, home-side atmosphere backdrop on the match header, real player faces on the Lineups pitch.

---

## Per-criterion

### 1. HIERARCHY — PASS
One hero: the Momentum River. The 48px header crests are identity markers flanking the score, the atmosphere sits at `opacity-10`, and the trade panel / Next Goal / Market Status rail read as secondary. No competing hero created by the crest/atmosphere additions.

### 2. TOKENS — PASS
Zero raw hex, zero `bg-[#…]`/`text-[#…]` in `TeamCrest.tsx` or any crest-swapped file. Kit colour is sourced from the jersey **image** (`teamMedia.ts` "kit — the source of the kit-colour accent"), NOT a per-team hex token — the token law holds. The atmosphere legibility scrim (`MatchHeader.tsx`) is `from-surface/70 via-surface/85 to-surface` — surface-token opacities only, the sanctioned legibility exception, not decorative. Numbers IBM Plex Mono + `tabular-nums`; prices one decimal.
- Minor pre-existing nit (not introduced here, not a fail): arbitrary `tracking-[…]` + `shadow-[0_0_5px_var(--up)]` glows in NextGoal/PickPad (glow colour is tokenized; blur is not).

### 3. RESTRAINT — PASS
Lineups / Stats / H2H / Events / Positions are all tabs; nothing tabbable is on the primary Market surface. The faces-on-pitch renders only when the Lineups tab is active. The atmosphere is ambient identity at `opacity-10`, not surfaced media.

### 4. BLEND — PASS
The match view keeps the Polymarket spine (header → River → trade ticket), dark/calm Hyperliquid feel, Sofascore density confined to discovery + tabs. Crests add football texture without turning the surface into a stats-spine.

### 5. MOTION — PASS
An imagery pass with **no new animation**. The atmosphere backdrop is a static `next/image` (correctly NOT an idle animation). Interactive transitions stay in the 150–250ms ease-out band, transform/opacity only. Atmosphere not `priority` (lazy), keyed on static `homeCode`, off the trading hot path.

### 6. STATES — PASS
Full hover/focus-visible/active on every interactive element touched; `TeamCrest` → `<Flag>` fallback (empty/unmatched), `Lineups` FacesPitch → DotPitch fallback (`squad.length >= 5`).

### 7. A11Y — PASS
Visible focus rings; atmosphere image `aria-hidden` + `alt=""`; crests `alt="{code} crest"`; faces `alt={player}`. axe 0 at rest and on every depth tab; two pre-existing `text-lo/70` contrast fails (Lineups caption + H2H comp label) promoted to full `text-lo`. Scrim keeps score/tape ≥4.5:1. Hit targets ≥44px.

### 8. COPY — PASS
Judged surfaces clean: Buy / Sell / Trade this match / credits / CR / Free to play / "Confirmed XI & formation pending live data" / "Play-money: no deposits, no payouts." No bet/stake/odds/wager/gamble on match/lineups/terminal/board.
- FLAG (off the judged surfaces): "odds" survived in one rendered string `/api/odds/stream` on `/how` (`TxLineSection.tsx`). → resolved post-verdict (see below).

### 9. CONSISTENCY — PASS (judged four); FLAG (app-wide split at time of judgment)
The four judged screens share one shell and are internally consistent — every team on board/terminal/match/lineups is a crest.
- FLAG: the same team still rendered as a flag disc on Portfolio/History/Proofs/MomentDetail/Bracket/GroupTable at judgment time. → resolved post-verdict (see below).

### 10. ELEVATION — PASS (beats the reference)
Reference rendered every team as a flag circle and Lineups as text. Impl swaps in real national crests (emblems, `object-contain`), adds a home-side atmosphere backdrop, and converts Lineups into real player faces on a native SVG pitch — including the goalscorer Ashour's actual face. Three independent axes of genuine elevation.

### 11. FEELING — PASS
A football fan who never trades would want to look at this. Named delight: the Lineups faces-on-a-pitch (real photos + kit-colour jersey accent + crest, honestly captioned "Squad"/"XI pending"), and the crest identity + home-side atmosphere behind the score.

### 12. PROVENANCE — PASS
Every visible non-Ninety primitive has a row with searches logged: `TeamCrest` (image lane, Flag fallback), MatchHeader atmosphere, Lineups faces pitch (sofascore-research hand-build, API-Football BLOCKED logged), app-wide crest swap. `hand-build` on the Lineups pitch is legal (football-depth surface, empty registry searches logged).
- FLAG: ADR-062 + PROVENANCE under-reported the diff at judgment time (said "board rails left on flags"). → resolved post-verdict (see below).

---

## OVERALL — PASS

All gate lines (1–9, 11, 12) PASS and line 10 is PASS (beats the reference). The richness pass is a real elevation: token-clean (crest/kit/atmosphere image-sourced, no per-team hex; scrim is the sanctioned surface-token exception), honest (atmosphere is team-not-venue with the venue as text; Lineups is a captioned squad, not a faked XI), restrained (depth stays tabbed), delightful (faces-on-pitch + crest identity). Ship it. Outstanding work is convergence, not correction.
