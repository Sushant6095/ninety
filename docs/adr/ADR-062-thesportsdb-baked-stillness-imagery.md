# ADR 062 TheSportsDB baked stillness imagery — crests, jerseys, atmosphere + player faces (a third stillness source, never a runtime call)

Status: accepted 2026-07-15 (`feat/richness`). Cross-ref: ADR-051 (WC26 two-source rule — TxLINE owns everything that MOVES, worldcup26 owns what SITS STILL), ADR-050 (consumer layer — screens read, never fetch), the existing baked-image precedents (`public/flags` via `scripts/bake-flags.mjs`, `/world-land.png`, `/sticker-play-money.png`), and `scripts/bake-lineups.mjs` (the API-Football stillness bake this sits beside).

Context: the app looked "basic" because every team appearance was a flag circle and the depth tabs were text-only. Real crests, kit jerseys, stadium atmosphere and player faces ALL sit still, so under ADR-051 they may come from a stillness source — provided they are baked offline and committed, never fetched at runtime. TheSportsDB's free public key ("3") supplies all four for national teams; it needs no signup. TxLINE remains the sole owner of scores/goals/halts/prices/results — this ADR adds imagery only.

## Decisions

- **TheSportsDB is a THIRD stillness source, under the same two-source law.** `scripts/bake-teams.mjs` fetches crest (`strBadge`), jersey (`strEquipment`), atmosphere (`strFanart1`) and ~10 player faces (`lookup_all_players.php` → `strThumb`) for all 48 WC26 teams (join: team name → TheSportsDB, senior-men filter excluding U17/women/futsal; aliases `COD→"DR Congo"`, `USA→"USA"`, `BIH→"Bosnia"` where the country name resolves wrong). Images land in `apps/web/public/teams/<wc26-id>/`; a committed manifest `apps/web/src/data/wc26/media.json` is IMPORTED (bundled) by the app. `assertNoMovement()` fails the bake if a movement field ever leaks in. **Zero runtime calls** to thesportsdb.com anywhere in `apps/web/src` (grep-proven); the bake is the only fetch, and the free key is public (never a secret, safe in a public repo).

- **`TeamCrest` is a safe drop-in for `<Flag>`.** It renders the baked crest free-standing (`object-contain` — a crest is an emblem, not a disc) and FALLS BACK to the circular `Flag` for any unmatched team, so every swap is regression-proof. Applied wherever a team is named, app-wide (one team = one treatment everywhere): FeaturedPanel + MatchHeader get BIG crests (52/48px, `priority`); MatchCard, H2H, the terminal market rail (CompetitionsRail), Open Positions, Next Goal, the pick pad, the board rails (LeftRail followed-teams, GroupStandings, PowerRankings, TopMovers) and the remaining team pages (Portfolio, History, Proofs, Moments, Bracket, Competition GroupTable) all get crests. Any team outside the baked 48 (e.g. an eliminated group-stage side referenced in history/proofs) falls back to the flag disc — honest, since only the 48 finalists are baked.

- **Match header opens with atmosphere, honestly.** The free tier has NO neutral-venue image (`strStadiumThumb` is empty; `searchvenues.php` returns nothing), so a MetLife/Lumen shot is unobtainable. Rather than fake it, the MatchHeader backdrop is the HOME side's `strFanart1` (team crowd/stadium) at `opacity-10` under a surface-token legibility scrim (the sanctioned exception to the no-gradient law — it fades the image back to the surface token so the score/tape hold ≥4.5:1; never decorative), and the venue NAME stays the authoritative text. It reads as match atmosphere, not a venue claim.

- **The Lineups tab renders the real squad on the native SVG pitch — no iframe, no empty tab.** API-Football (formation + confirmed XI, which MOVE with team news) is BLOCKED with no key. So the pitch shows the TheSportsDB SQUAD (real names + faces, bucketed by position, coach as a chip), captioned "Confirmed XI & formation pending live data (API-Football)". It falls back to the illustrative dot-pitch for any unmatched side. The jersey renders beside the crest as the kit-colour accent (image-sourced — no per-team hex token, so the token law holds).

## Consequences / VERIFY
`tsc --noEmit` clean; no new runtime deps (`apps/web/package.json` unchanged — bake uses Node's global `fetch`). axe **0** on `/`, `/board`, `/terminal`, `/match/[id]` at rest AND on every `/match` depth tab (Stats/Lineups/H2H/Events/Positions/Market) — two pre-existing `text-lo/70` contrast fails (Lineups caption + H2H comp label) fixed to full `text-lo` along the way. Playwright-verified: 47/48 teams have crest+jersey+atmosphere+~10 faces (Jordan has 0 players on the free tier — an honest coverage gap; crest+jersey still present); the Lineups pitch shows 18 real faces (AUS+EGY, incl. the goalscorer Ashour). Footprint: 613 committed files, ~100 MB (78 MB is the all-48 player photos, per the explicit bake-all instruction; no single file >504 KB).

BLOCKED (needs a human credential): **`API_FOOTBALL_KEY`** — with it, `scripts/bake-lineups.mjs` bakes the confirmed formation + starting XI and API-Football supplies real H2H/stats; without it the squad-faces pitch and the illustrative H2H stand in honestly. A runtime `fetch()` to thesportsdb.com / api-sports.io from `apps/web/src`, or a hotlinked (non-`public/`) team image, is a bug.
